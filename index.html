About

Who Eats Off Of The Clothes On Your Back? is an interactive web-based platform developed by Jingwen Zhu and Ashley Cortez to improve transparency in our buying habits in the apparel and textile industries.

Who Eats Off Of The Clothes On Your Back? works by leveraging data from The International Labour Organization, individual company’s open data and national food costs and synthesizes them into what a day in the life of someone who makes your garments is afforded to live by.

In the future, Jingwen and Ashley would like to add more company’s information and have a broader reach in order to evoke empathy and help others be mindful with their buying power.
